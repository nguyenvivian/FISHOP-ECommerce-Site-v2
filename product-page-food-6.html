Detailed Description: Origin: This lobster's ancestors lived across a wide region in Australia, where they were raised for food in large ponds. Mostly colored black, brown, and orange, but eventually a few blue ones were collected and bred. The resulting Blue Moon Lobsters quickly became popular in aquariums. Maximum Size: In aquariums, this lobster often grows to be about 6" long, but they live many years and continue to grow slowly for their entire lives. Behaviors: They usually stay at the bottom of the tank, and have quite a personality.
